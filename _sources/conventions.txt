Angle conventions
=================

GGADT supports two different angle conventions. You can tell GGADT which one to use by specifying the :option:`--axes-convention` parameter as either ``MSTM`` or ``DDSCAT``.

DDSCAT
^^^^^^

Same conventions that are used in the `DDSCAT`_ code:
