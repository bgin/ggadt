.. _conventions:

Angle conventions
=================

GGADT supports two different angle conventions. The first is the same angle conventions that are used in the `DDSCAT`_ code:

.. figure:: ddscat_orientations.png
	:width: 600px
	:align: center

	DDSCAT conventions

The second is that used in the `MSTM`_ code:

.. figure:: mackowski_orientations.png
	:width: 600px
	:align: center

	MSTM conventions

You can tell GGADT which one to use by specifying the ``--axes-convention`` parameter as either ``mstm`` or ``ddscat``.

.. _DDSCAT : http://www.ddscat.org/
.. _MSTM : http://eng.auburn.edu/users/dmckwski/scatcodes/
