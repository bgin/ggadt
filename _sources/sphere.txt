Sphere
======

For :index:`spherical particles`, :program:`GGADT` solves an integral expression for :math:`S(\theta)` and converts this to :math:`dC_{\rm sca}/d\Omega` (see [DA2006]_, [HD2015]_, [VDH1957]_). You can force GGADT to use the general solution by passing the :option:`--force-numerical` flag (alternatively, you can set :option:`force-numerical=T` in your parameter file).
